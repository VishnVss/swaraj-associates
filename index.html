About the Firm

A Partnership Law Firm with 30 years of expertise — conducting and succeeding cases in High Courts, District Courts, Consumer Courts, Labour Courts, DRT, and DRAT since 1996.

Swaraj Associates is a trusted full-service Partnership Law Firm in Chennai, with its chamber at the High Court Campus — serving corporates, financial institutions, builders, and individuals across Tamil Nadu since 1996.

Founded by M.P. Rajendran (Enrollment No. Ms. 851/96) after training under Government Pleader Mr. K.P.H. Thulasiraman at Madras High Court and with M/s. Rank Associates — Panel Counsel for SBI, Canara Bank, BOI, HDFC, ICICI, Standard Chartered and Citi Bank. Partner B. Eswaran (Enrollment No. Ms. 1152/96) brings 30 years of complementary civil and criminal expertise.

With 5 legal professionals, the firm covers corporate advisory, real estate, IT law, taxation, banking recovery, employment, and family matters — delivering complete end-to-end legal solutions.

Proven Results

Success Stories

Three cases showcasing our creative, results-focused approach to legal strategy.

1st Success Story · Money Recovery
Rs. 50 Lakh Recovered in 3 Months via Strategic Pleadings

Andavar Nidhi Ltd. extended a Rs. 50 lakh loan with only a luxury car's records as security and a vague agreement. When the borrower defaulted, the firm chose an "Under Chapter Suit" — drafting pleadings that correlated the borrowing with the car records, making the borrower completely defenceless. A court order was obtained within 3 months. The borrower repaid full principal and interest through court.

Full recovery within 3 months
2nd Success Story · Real Estate
Multi-Crore Land Sale Saved in One Week Without Litigation

Whites India's 3-acre sale to G-Square was threatened by a 1973 survey number mismatch. In law, conveyance deed rectification is permissible only within 3 years — after that, only a court can correct it (minimum 1 year). The buyer could not wait. The firm registered a declaration by the present owner at the Sub-Registrar within one week, legally rectifying the defect. The sale was completed on time.

Transaction saved in 1 week
3rd Success Story · Employment Law
Watertight Employment Contract Defeats Police Complaint

Technocrat Solutions faced trained employees jumping to rivals. The firm drafted contracts with 2-year minimum service clauses, 6-month probation, collected original certificates, signed cheques from surety & father, and indemnity bonds. When an employee filed a complaint at Arumbakkam Police Station, the firm produced the contract. Police accepted the firm's version, closed the case. Employee paid full compensation as per contract.

Case closed, compensation recovered
